## 🎯 First Steps

1. **Install** - Follow [GET_STARTED.md](GET_STARTED.md)
2. **Run** - Execute `start.bat` or `./start.sh`
3. **Create Course** - Click "Courses" → Create "Machine Learning"
4. **Upload Document** - Go to "AI Tutor" → Upload `backend/test_ml.txt`
5. **Ask Question** - Type "What is machine learning?"
6. **Explore** - Try flashcards, quizzes, summaries!
